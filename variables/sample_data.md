# Sales Data Q1 2024

## Regional Performance

### North America
- Revenue: $2.4M (↑ 15% YoY)
- New Customers: 245
- Churn Rate: 3.2%
- Top Product: Enterprise Plan (65% of revenue)

### Europe
- Revenue: $1.8M (↑ 22% YoY)
- New Customers: 189
- Churn Rate: 2.8%
- Top Product: Professional Plan (58% of revenue)

### Asia Pacific
- Revenue: $1.1M (↑ 45% YoY)
- New Customers: 156
- Churn Rate: 4.1%
- Top Product: Starter Plan (52% of revenue)

## Key Insights
- Overall revenue growth of 24% year-over-year
- Enterprise segment showing strongest growth
- Customer acquisition cost decreased by 12%
- Average deal size increased from $18K to $23K
